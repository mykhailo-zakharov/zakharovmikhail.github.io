Shorena Kravelidze

Region: Shida Kartli

City: Kurta

Age: 30

"My name is Shorena Kravelidze. I am 39 years old.

From the beginning I was not able to attend school, so my teachers were visiting me at home. I had a negative attitude about studying; I wanted to study with the other children. I wanted to be at school. By 7th grade I realized the importance of education, but it was too late. I was so far behind that I couldn't keep up with the program.

After that we moved to Marneuli. At first I didn't even want to go out of the house. I went through a terrible period of depression but finally managed to overcome it. I actively searched for a job, telling everyone I was looking, and found one working at a newspaper stand.

After working there for 8 years I realized I needed to find a different job and started thinking about progress. I did my best to get a better job but all the employers denied my applications because I did not have any education or diploma.

I did not want to accept things as they were and decided to fight for my goals. That's why, at the age of 39, I decided to started studying. I am a student of management now. I will graduate soon and hope that I will get a better job."
