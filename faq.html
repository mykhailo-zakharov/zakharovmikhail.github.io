Why launchcircle

Is launchcircle right for me?

Do you have a great idea that you would like actualize? Then yes!

At what stages can launchcircle be incorporated into my project?

We help you bring your idea to life, whether that means deﬁning features or simply building features. As long as you have a product concept, we can offer our expertise to help shape the rest.

How do I know that my ideas are safe with you?

Our conﬁdentiality agreement gives you full legal rights to your own ideas so that you feel comfortable elaborating as much as necessary to best bring your idea to life.

How much does launchcircle cost?

Different projects have different requirements. By ﬁlling out a simple form under „Get a quote“, we can give you a personalized price estimation for your project. This is free and requires no commitment.

My team

How does launchcircle build my team?

Our project manager handpicks the team to align with project requirements after deﬁning them with you. This process ensures that the most suitible talent works on your project.

How do I know that the developers who tackle my project are suited for the job

Our teams are handpicked to best ﬁt each project need. But don’t just take our word for it - check out some of our awesome customer testimonials!

Why do I have to communicate to the team through a project manager?

Our project managers are able to advise you on any unforseen questions and present your feedback in a way that is most easily recieved by our engineers. Our experiences have shown that this system faciliates the greatest success all around!

I have engineering knowledge. Is a project manager really necessary?

Your engineering knowledge will certainly be a great beneﬁt to the project. However, streamlining our approach by using the same process in our projects is one of the key factors that allows us to complete your project in such a timely manner.
